<p><strong>Gameplay Mechanics</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Essence and Perks</strong>: Players earn Essence through kills, which can be spent on upgrades and perks. A default knife allows for early-game kills without wasting ammo.</li>
<li><strong>Training Technique</strong>: Keeping a few zombies alive at the end of a round allows players to complete objectives without triggering new rounds. This technique is essential for managing the chaos of zombie hordes.</li>
